Components of a Computer

cpu

What Is a CPU? (Central Processing Unit) A CPU (Central Processing Unit) is the "brain" of a computer, a crucial electronic component that executes instructions from software and controls other parts, performing calculations, logic, and data management to turn input into usable information, working in a fetch-decode-execute cycle.

consist of:-

  1. arithmetic loical unit
  2. control unit
  3. register

motherboard

A motherboard is the main circuit board in a computer, acting as the central hub that connects and allows communication between all essential internal components


card slot

A card slot is a physical opening or socket in electronic devices (like phones, cameras, laptops) designed to hold and read various types of memory or identification cards
